OUR SERVICES

At Bright Haven Home, we believe digital safety should be simple, affordable, and effective.

CORE SERVICES

01

Device Setup

We configure smartphones, tablets, laptops, gaming consoles, and other devices with parental controls, privacy settings, and content filters. This includes setting age-appropriate screen time limits and blocking dangerous websites.

Why it matters:

Most devices are wide open out of the box — leaving kids one search away from harmful content. Our setup closes those gaps.

02

Account Setup

We configure Netflix, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, gaming profiles, and other accounts to enforce age-appropriate content settings, privacy rules, and safe search modes.

Why it matters:

Many popular platforms make it easy for kids to bypass restrictions — we lock them down and make sure they stay that way.

03

Home Network Filtering

We install a dedicated filtering device in your home network to block harmful sites and ads on every device connected to your Wi-Fi — without slowing your internet.

Why it matters:

Even unprotected guest devices or smart TVs can access unsafe content. Network filtering adds a home-wide layer of protection.

04

Child Identity Protection Support

Most parents don’t realize their child could already have a credit file. We lock it down now, so no one can take out a loan in their name before they even turn 18.

Why it matters:

Children are prime targets for identity theft, and stolen Social Security numbers often go undetected until years later— leaving families with financial and legal nightmares to untangle.

05

Digital Wellness Session

A one-hour guided session with the family to review how technology is being used at home and open up healthy conversations about online safety.​​

Why it matters:

Families need a safe space to step back from the daily tech grind, talk openly about online challenges, and get expert guidance. These check-ins help parents stay connected, kids feel heard, and everyone build healthier, safer digital habits together.

ADDITIONAL SERVICES

01

Secured Storage Setup

We build and install a private in-home storage system with terabytes of secure space for your family’s photos and files. This gives you cloud convenience without monthly fees.

02

Parental Monitoring Setup

We install and configure a leading parental monitoring tool, to help you track texts, apps, and social media for warning signs like cyberbullying, predators, or explicit content. Includes ongoing support so alerts are reviewed and settings stay up to date.

03

VPN Setup

We install and configure a secure Virtual Private Network (VPN) to keep your family’s browsing private, protect data on public Wi-Fi, and add an extra layer of online safety. Once set up, your devices will automatically connect through the VPN for safer, more private internet use.

04

Password Vault Setup

We create and configure a family password manager that stores all your logins securely in one place, making it easy to share access safely across devices. This service also includes guidance on how to use the vault for strong, unique passwords that protect against hackers.​​

FAQ

What exactly does Bright Haven Home do?

We help parents create a safer digital environment for their family. That includes setting up device controls, filtering harmful content, limiting screen time, and offering ongoing support so your kids can enjoy tech safely.

I’m not tech-savvy. Can I still do this?

Absolutely. We specialize in helping busy, non-techy parents. We explain everything in plain English and do most of the setup for you—so you feel confident, not confused.

What happens after I book the free consultation?

You’ll receive a confirmation email with a Zoom link. During the session, we’ll get to know each other and determine if it's a good fit to partner together in your journey of protecting your family. If it's a good fit, we'll either book a Digital Wellness Session or draft an invoice for services we recommend.

Do you work with schools or community groups?

We offer workshops, presentations, and partnerships with local organizations. Contact us for more info.

What devices can you help with?

We support phones, tablets, computers, smart TVs, gaming consoles, and home Wi-Fi routers. If it connects to the internet, we can help protect it.

My kids are older and tech-savvy. Will this still work?

Yes. Our solutions are built for all ages—from curious 5-year-olds to digital-native teens. We help you stay one step ahead and create healthy digital habits at any age.

What if I need help later?

That’s why we offer our Digital Wellness Sessions! You’ll get the support, including check-ins, tech tweaks, and assistance in facilitating meaningful conversations with your family.

Will my internet slow down or stop working?

No. We configure safety filters without affecting your internet speed or functionality. Most families don’t notice any difference in performance—just better peace of mind.

Can I customize what my kids can or can’t do?

Definitely. We’ll tailor controls to match your family values—whether you want to block specific apps, set bedtime rules, or just monitor usage.

Is my family’s data or privacy at risk?

Absolutely not. We never access your personal data. Our tools are built with privacy and security in mind. You're in control, and we’ll walk you through every setting.

What’s included in the free 30-minute consultation?

We’ll assess your current tech setup, understand your concerns, and provide personalized advice on making your home digitally safer. No obligation—just guidance from a parent-focused expert with a digital safety plan tailored to meet your family's needs.

How long does the setup take?

Setup typically takes 60–90 minutes depending on your devices and needs. We schedule everything at a time that works for your family.

Do you sell any products or apps?

We don't create our own products/apps. We’re not here to push products. Our job is to help you use what you already have (phones, Wi-Fi, laptops) in a safer and smarter way.

How is this different from just using parental controls on my own?

We bring deep experience, expert tools, and time-saving guidance. Most parents spend hours trying to figure things out—then give up. We simplify everything and ensure it’s done right the first time.
